# **FINAL BOSS CONCEPT**

Rather than having the final boss speak, make it a manifestation of all the emotional burdens the protagonist has been carrying. Its silence makes it more unsettling because the previous entities have already voiced every fear, doubt, and regret. The player instinctively understands what it represents without being told.

This structure gives each route a distinct emotional focus while maintaining a consistent gameplay loop. Burnout embodies relentless pressure, Regret anchors the past, Lost questions identity and the future, and Deprivation mourns what has been sacrificed. By the time the final chase begins, the horror feels personal rather than random, making the transition to the semi-3D sequence feel like the emotional climax instead of just an action set piece.

Silence.

Everything stops.

Footsteps.

No dialogue.
